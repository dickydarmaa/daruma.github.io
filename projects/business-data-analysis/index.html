üéØ Strategic Recommendations

  • Urgent: Investigate 2023 Decline - The 87% revenue drop in 2023 requires immediate root cause analysis. Examine external factors (economic conditions, competition) and internal operations (supply chain, marketing effectiveness).
  • Restock High-Demand Items - Immediately replenish Scarf inventory (currently 53 units). Monitor Sweater and Celana Jeans stock levels closely given their high sales volume.
  • Optimize Inventory Mix - Reduce overstock on Jaket and Topi (182 units each) through promotional pricing or bundle offers. Redirect capital to fast-moving products.
  • Strengthen Regional Marketing - Prioritize advertising budgets in top-performing cities (Semarang, Makassar, Medan). Investigate growth opportunities in underperforming markets like Yogyakarta and Surabaya.
  • Targeted Demographic Campaigns - Focus marketing on 30-60 age demographic, particularly women aged 50-60 who show highest purchasing power. Develop age-appropriate messaging and product placement.
  • Product Portfolio Review - Evaluate low-performers (Tas, Topi) for pricing adjustments or discontinuation. Consider introducing complementary products that appeal to core demographic.
  • Seasonal Promotion Strategy - Implement strategic discounts during historically low-sales periods to smooth revenue fluctuations and improve cash flow consistency.
  • Bundle Strategy - Create product bundles combining high-margin items (Sweater, Celana Jeans) with slow-movers (Tas, Topi) to increase average transaction value and clear excess inventory.
